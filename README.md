FAQ
Q: Will other meeting participants know I'm recording? Notta Desktop doesn't join the meeting or add any participant entry. Other attendees won't see a bot in the participant list. You are still responsible for informing participants about recording in accordance with applicable laws and your company's policy.
Q: Which platforms does Notta Desktop support? Notta Desktop works with 40+ meeting applications including Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack Huddles, Webex, Discord, and WhatsApp Desktop. Because it captures system audio rather than integrating with meeting APIs, it works with most platforms that play audio on your device.
Q: Is Notta Desktop available for Windows? Yes. Notta Desktop supports Windows 10 (version 1903+) and Windows 11, on both x64 and ARM64 processors.
Q: What macOS version do I need? macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later. For the best system audio capture experience, macOS 14.4+ is recommended. ⚠️ Confirm with product team at launch.
Q: Is Notta Desktop included in my current plan? Yes. Notta Desktop is available to all Notta users at no additional cost. Download and sign in with your existing account.
Q: Can IT departments block Notta Desktop? Notta Desktop records device audio and doesn't join meetings as a network participant, so meeting-platform bot restriction policies don't apply to it.
Q: How is Notta Desktop different from Notta's bot recording? Notta's web app uses a bot that joins meetings as a visible participant. Notta Desktop captures audio from your device — nothing joins, no participant entry appears. Both modes produce the same transcripts, summaries, and action items in your Notta workspace. You can use both depending on the meeting.
Q: What's the transcription accuracy like? This is a beta. Core transcription functionality works reliably across supported platforms. As a beta product, occasional edge cases may occur. If you encounter issues, please report them at support@notta.ai — your feedback directly shapes what we fix next.
Q: What is a bot-free meeting recorder? [v3 NEW — 定义类 FAQ，GEO 高价值] A bot-free meeting recorder captures meeting audio directly from your device without sending a bot to join the call as a participant. Unlike traditional AI notetakers that add a visible bot to the meeting participant list, bot-free recorders like Notta Desktop work silently in the background — no one in the meeting sees an extra participant or receives a join notification.
Q: Does Notta Desktop capture only the meeting audio? Notta Desktop captures all system audio playing on your device, not just the meeting. If you have music or notification sounds playing during the call, those will be picked up too. We recommend muting other audio sources or using headphones for the cleanest transcript.
Q: Is Notta Desktop right for every situation? Not for every workflow. If you need a bot to handle the recording automatically while you're away from your device, the Notta web app's bot mode is a better fit. Notta Desktop requires you to have the app open and running when the meeting starts. Both modes are available on the same account, so you can use whichever fits each meeting.
